## 1. The "Leads Dashboard" (Sales/Lead Coordinator Portal)

*Target User: Corey (Lead Coordinator), Salespeople.*\
*Core Philosophy: Gamification, Focus, Speed-to-Lead.*

### **A. The "Heads-Up Display" (HUD) & Queue Logic**

- **The "Focus Mode" Interface:**
  - A single monitor view dedicated solely to this dashboard.
  - **Visual Notification System:**
    - "Ding" sound effect on new lead arrival.
    - Visual takeover: A "New Lead" card appears (e.g., top right or center screen) demanding immediate attention.
  - **Countdown Timers:**
    - Individual timers per lead card (indicating time since arrival/staleness).
    - Timer resets/pauses based on activity.
- **Smart Queue Prioritization (AI-Driven):**
  - **"New Lead" State:** Takes absolute priority. Must drop everything to call.
  - **"Touch These Leads" State:** Queue of follow-ups.
  - **"Background" State:** Leads that fade into the background after a set time (e.g., 1 minute).
  - **Re-Engagement Logic:**
    - If no new leads, surface older leads needing touchpoints.
    - **Smart Scheduling:** AI suggests *when* to call based on contact rates (e.g., "This lead hasn't answered M-F 8-5, try Saturday 10-12" or "Weekday 6-7 PM").
    - Prioritize double-dials (e.g., call in AM, cue to call again in PM).

### **B. Lead Action Cards**

- **Contextual Scripting:**
  - Dynamic script display based on lead source/funnel (e.g., "Fit Funnel Script" vs. "Estimator Tool Script").
  - Dynamic script display based on lead stage (e.g., "Booked but needs confirmation" vs. "Unbooked").
  - 3/6/9 month re-engagment scripts.
- **Auto-Fill Data:** Lead contact info pre-populated.
- **Quick Actions:**
  - Click-to-dial.
  - One-click Text (preset templates).
  - One-click Email (preset templates).
  - Voicemail Drop scripts.
- **Outcome Logging (Granular Disposition):**
  - Dropdown options for call result:
    - Out of service area.
    - Never answered.
    - Looking for job (applicant).
    - Looking for service.
    - Already had estimate.
    - "Don't remember filling out form."

### **C. Gamification & Compensation Tracking**

- **Real-Time Commission Ticker:**
  - Visual "Bing!" and cash animation (e.g., +$20) immediately upon booking a lead.
  - **Commission Logic:**
    - Flat fee per booked lead (e.g., $20).
    - **Tiered Bonuses:** Extra commission based on *Booking Rate* percentage (e.g., maintain &gt;X% booking rate for bonus).
    - Potential for "Sold" commission tracking (if data allows).
- **Salesperson Performance Stats:**
  - Speed-to-lead timer tracking.
  - Volume metrics: # Calls made, # Voicemails left, # bookings, booking rate

### **D. Lead Attribution & categorization**

- **Automated Inputs:**
  - Lead Source (e.g., Meta, Google, Organic).
  - Lead Type (e.g., Fit Funnel, Quiz, Pop-up).
  - Interest Type (Air Conditioner, Heat Pump, Ductless, Full System, Furnace).
  - Old or New Customer
- **Status Management:**
  - Booked (Online Scheduler vs. Manual Book).
  - Confirmed (for Online Scheduler leads).
  - Sold (Revenue attribution).
  - Lost/Cancelled.

## 2. The Client Portal (Owner/Manager View)

*Target User: Brandon, Dan, HVAC Business Owners.*\
*Core Philosophy: Transparency, Real-time Decision Making, "True" ROI.*

### **A. The "Searchlight Killer" Analytics Dashboard**

- **Core Metrics (The "Big 5"):**
  - Cost Per Lead (CPL) - *Must break down by category/campaign.*
  - Booking Rate %.
  - Close Rate %.
  - Average Sale Value ($).
  - ROI (Return on Investment) per lead created.
- **Granular Drill-Downs:**
  - Filter by **Campaign Type / Promo** (e.g., Fit Funnel specifically).
  - **Salesperson Performance:**
    - Close rate *by* Lead Type (e.g., "Taden's close rate on Heat Pump leads").
    - Booking outcome by Lead Coordinator.
- **Timeline & "Change Log" Overlay:**
  - **Feature:** A graphical overlay on performance charts marking *when* a marketing change occurred.
  - **Data Point:** "Feb 10: Overhauled Meta Ad Set."
  - **Visual:** Click the marker to see the description of the change, correlated with spikes/dips in Booking Rate/CPL.
- **Revenue Accuracy (Solved):**
  - Track "Sold" Revenue (Signed Estimates) vs. "Cash Collected" (avoid 30-day delays in reporting).
  - Integration with Service Titan to pull signed estimates immediately.

### **B. Financial Transparency**

- **"True ROI" Toggle:**
  - Option to include **Agency Fees** (e.g., the $5k/mo retainer) into the ROI calculation.
  - View 1: Ad Spend ROI (ROAS).
  - View 2: "All Costs" ROI (Ad Spend + Agency Fees).
- **Budget Pacing:**
  - "Projected Spend" Graph.
  - Math: (Current Spend / Days Elapsed) \* Total Days in Month.
  - Visual indicator of trending over/under budget.

### **C. Proactive Intelligence & Training (LMS)**

- **Bottleneck Identification:**
  - System highlights the weak point (e.g., "Booking Rate is down to 15%").
- **Embedded Training (The "Upsell Engine"):**
  - Contextual Content: If Booking Rate is low, show "Free Tips to Improve Booking Rate."
  - Direct Upsell: "Buy the Advanced Booking Rate Course" button directly in the dashboard.
  - Email Automation: Trigger emails to the owner when metrics dip, containing specific training/upsell content.

### **D. Usability Fixes (vs. Searchlight)**

- **Comparison Ranges:**
  - *Requirement:* Allow "No Comparison" view. (Don't force red arrows during shoulder season when comparing to peak season).
  - Compare against industry benchmarks or "Agency Average" (see Internal Portal).
- **Semantic Labeling:** Use the client's internal verbiage for funnels (e.g., "Fit Funnel") not generic "Facebook Lead Form."
- **Semantic Filtering:** Ask in natural language what they want to see (e.g., "Show me all meta campaigns and cpl and total spend" - or - "hide all google and organic"). Allows you to manually and automatically save as a custimizable quick view. And auto-suggest the next filter query based on historical patterns for that particular user.
- **Customizable Quick Views:** Ability to hide irrelevant metrics (reduce overwhelm).
- **Chat Interface:** "Ask your data questions" (e.g., "Why did my CPL go up last week?"). Shows suggested questions based on real-time data and saved questions from the user.

## 4. The Admin Portal & Architecture

*Target User: Aaron (Operator).*\
*Core Philosophy: Management of the ecosystem, The "Golden Record".*

### **A. User & Client Management**

- **Client Onboarding:** Setup new client instances.
- **User Roles:**
  - Super Admin (Aaron/Partners).
  - Agency User (YooJin/Ben).
  - Client Admin (Owner).
  - Client User (Salesperson/Corey).

### **B. The Attribution Engine (The "Middleware")**

*The central nervous system for matching Ad Spend to ServiceTitan Revenue.*

#### **1. The "Join Key" Hierarchy (The Waterfall)**

- **Level 1 (Diamond):** `GCLID` / `WBRAID` (Click ID) — 100% Accuracy.
- **Level 2 (Golden):** `hashed_phone_number` + `timestamp` — 90% Accuracy.
- **Level 3 (Silver):** `hashed_email` — 80% Accuracy.
- **Level 4 (Bronze):** `billing_address` (Normalized) — Use for Household matching (e.g., Wife clicks, Husband calls).

#### **2. Capture Layer (Client-Side)**

- **"Cookie Jar" Script:**
  - Parses URL params (`?gclid=`, `?utm_source=`).
  - Writes to **First-Party Cookie** (90-day life) & `localStorage`.
- **Form Injection:**
  - Uses `MutationObserver` to watch for scheduling widgets (ServiceTitan/Schedule Engine).
  - Silently injects `GCLID` into hidden fields.
- **Phone Call Bridge:**
  - DNI (Dynamic Number Insertion) via CallRail/Twilio.
  - Captures `GCLID` for the session and fires a webhook on call completion.

#### **3. Ingest Layer (Server-Side)**

- **ServiceTitan Custom Fields:**
  - Group: `Attribution_Meta`.
  - Fields: `Attribution_GCLID` (Primary Key), `Attribution_Source`, `Attribution_Landing_Page`, `Attribution_Timestamp`.
- **Webhook Listener:**
  - Listens for DNI/Form webhooks.
  - **Action:** Calls ServiceTitan API (`POST/PATCH /jobs`) to inject the `GCLID` into the specific job.

#### **4. Reconciliation Layer (The "Truth" Cron Job)**

- **Schedule:** Runs nightly (e.g., 3:00 AM).
- **Process:**
  1. Fetch `Completed` jobs with `Revenue > 0`.
  2. Fetch Ad Spend (Google/Meta APIs).
  3. **Match Logic:**
     - Direct Hit (Job has `GCLID`).
     - DNI Fallback (Match Phone # in DNI logs -&gt; Get `GCLID`).
     - Household Match (Match normalized `Service Location` address -&gt; DNI logs).

#### **5. Feedback Loop (OCI)**

- **Offline Conversion Import:**
  - Formats confirmed revenue into Google Ads API `UploadClickConversions` payload.
  - Sends: `GCLID`, `Conversion Name` ("ServiceTitan_Installation_Revenue"), `Value` ($15k), `Time`.
- **Fallbacks:**
  - iOS 14 / Privacy Blocks: Use Google Enhanced Conversions (SHA-256 hashed email/phone).

### **C. Integrations Required**

- **ServiceTitan:**
  - API V2 access.
  - Endpoints: `/crm/v2/tenant/{id}/jobs` (POST/PATCH), Reporting API.
- **GoHighLevel (GHL):**
  - Pop-up forms & Shadow DOM tracking.
- **Meta (Facebook/Instagram):**
  - Marketing API (Spend, Campaigns).
  - Conversions API (CAPI) for server-side event match.
- **Google Ads:**
  - Google Ads API (Spend, OCI Uploads).
- **CallRail / Podium:**
  - Webhooks for call completion and review sentiment.
- **Stripe:**
  - Payment events for direct-sale products.
